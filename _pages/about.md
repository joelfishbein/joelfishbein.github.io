Summary
------
I am currently a postdoctoral psychology fellow at the VA San Diego, and am studying pain and its treatment. Previously, I completed my clinical psychology doctorate in Dr. Joanna Arch's lab at the University of Colorado Boulder, and my predoctoral internship at the University of California San Diego.

My Research
------
- How can we improve psychotherapy and other treatments for chronic pain?
- Why do psychotherapies work differently for different people?
- What are the mechanisms of psychotherapies? In other words, how do they help people to make meaningful changes in their lives?

You can view my research publications at [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YOURID).

My Clinical Work
------
As a clinician, I specialize in Cognitive Behavioral Therapies, including Acceptance and Commitment Therapy and mindfulness-based approaches. My work with each of my clients is a collaboration. I tailor my approach with each client to their preferences and circumstances. I have trained at the VA, the University of Colorado Anschutz, and UC San Diego.
